Parmesantupp

Description

This is an absolute amazing chicken meal. The saltiness of the parmesan with the spinach is a marvelous combo.

Serve it with rice and dont be cheap on the gravy. You should soak the rice with gravy!

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken fillet
  • 3 dl heavy cream
  • 150 g parmesan
  • 200 g spinach
  • 0,5 dl olive oil
  • 0,5 dl soy
  • 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in the soy and olive oil. Squeeze the lemon juice in to the marinade.
  2. Fry the chicken in a really hot pan until it gets some nice color.
  3. Put the chicken in an oven form
  4. Pour the cream over the chicken. Sprinkel parmesan and spinach over the chicken
  5. Put in oven at 200 degrees celsius for about 40 minutes
  6. Boil the rice and voila. Dig in!
